About the Branch

About Our
Student Branch

IEEE AUK SB is the official student branch of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) at Amity University Kolkata. We bring together students passionate about technology, engineering, and innovation.

As part of the world's largest technical professional organization, our branch organizes workshops, hackathons, seminars, and outreach programs that bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world engineering challenges.
