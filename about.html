Who We Are

About IEEE AUK SB

Our history, mission, and connection to the global IEEE network.

01

IEEE Global

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. With over 400,000 members in more than 160 countries, IEEE is the trusted voice for engineering, computing, and technology information around the globe.

IEEE publishes nearly a third of the world's technical literature in electrical engineering, electronics, and computer science and has pioneered many of the standards that underpin modern technology — from wireless communications to power systems and beyond.

Through its 39 Societies and 7 technical Councils, IEEE covers a broad range of technical interests. IEEE members include engineers, scientists, and allied professionals who share a commitment to advancing the field for the betterment of society.

02

IEEE Region 10

IEEE Region 10, also known as the Asia-Pacific Region, is the largest IEEE region by geographical area, spanning across Asia and the Pacific Rim. It includes countries such as India, China, Japan, South Korea, Australia, and many more across Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.

Region 10 has a vibrant community of IEEE members, student branches, and chapters working together to promote technical excellence, professional development, and community engagement throughout the Asia-Pacific region. The region organizes flagship conferences including TENCON and various symposia each year.

IEEE AUK SB is part of Region 10 and participates in regional competitions, conferences, and initiatives organized under this umbrella.

03

IEEE India Council

The IEEE India Council is one of the most active councils under IEEE Region 10, representing a vast and growing community of IEEE members across India. With over 65,000 members and more than 500 student branches, India has one of the largest IEEE communities in the world.

The IEEE India Council coordinates activities across various sections, organizes national-level technical conferences, student competitions, and professional development programs. It serves as the liaison between IEEE global and the Indian IEEE community, ensuring members have access to world-class resources and opportunities.

IEEE AUK SB falls under the IEEE Kolkata Section which falls under the IEEE India Council's jurisdiction and actively participates in India Council events, competitions, and professional activities.

04

IEEE AUK SB

The IEEE Amity University Kolkata Student Branch (IEEE AUK SB) was established in 2025 under the Kolkata Section Chapter to foster a culture of technical excellence, innovation, and professional development among students at Amity University Kolkata. Our student branch provides a platform for students to connect with the global engineering community.

We organize a wide range of activities including technical workshops, hackathons, seminars by industry professionals, research paper competitions, and community outreach programs. Our active chapters in Computer Science, Robotics & Automation, Signal Processing, and Women in Engineering ensure students from diverse engineering disciplines have a home in IEEE AUK SB.

The branch is guided by experienced faculty advisors and led by an enthusiastic executive committee of student volunteers who work year-round to create meaningful learning and networking opportunities for our members.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission
