AICSSYC 2025 — All India Computer Society Student & Youth Congress

01 September 2025 • Amity University Kolkata Auditorium

The All India Computer Society Student & Youth Congress (AICSSYC) is a flagship IEEE Computer Society gathering that brings together students, researchers, and industry professionals from across India.

The event focuses on knowledge sharing, innovation, networking, and collaboration among future technology leaders through technical talks, competitions, and interactive sessions.

Highlights of the Event

  • Keynote sessions from leading IEEE Computer Society members
  • Technical talks on Artificial Intelligence and Cloud Computing
  • Student innovation showcase
  • Networking opportunities with industry experts
  • Interactive panel discussion on future tech careers

Who Attended

Participants included IEEE student members, faculty researchers, industry mentors, and technology enthusiasts from universities across India.

Key Topics Covered

  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Cybersecurity and Ethical Hacking
  • Cloud Computing and Distributed Systems
  • Data Science and Big Data Analytics
  • Future of Computing Careers

About IEEE Computer Society

The IEEE Computer Society is the world's leading membership organization dedicated to computer science and technology. Through events like AICSSYC, it fosters collaboration and knowledge exchange among students and professionals globally.
